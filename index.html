My Work

Calm the F(ood) Down is a delightful 2D cooking game where players take on the role of a chef fulfilling customer orders. The challenge lies in preparing the necessary ingredients, cooking them to perfection, and serving the dishes promptly. What sets this game apart is its unique approach to difficulty—instead of the typical life system or timer, players face the intriguing twist of managing a stress level threshold. Success hinges on your ability to keep your cool and manage stress amidst the chaos of a bustling kitchen. Calm the F(ood) Down not only offers an enjoyable gaming experience but also serves as a valuable lesson in stress management within a dynamic and engaging environment.
We've developed a comprehensive time management mobile application designed to help users effectively track their activities, including focusing, cooking, exercising, meditating, and coding. Our app not only offers pre-defined categories but also empowers users to create custom tasks and monitor their time allocation. Much like the 'Screen Time' feature on iOS devices, our application generates insightful weekly graphs that showcase how users spend their time on various tasks.
