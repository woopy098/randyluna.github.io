Full Stack Developer

The app allows information to be posted by Indigenous Women’s organizations, and downloaded when users are online for offline viewing. My Work

Calm the F(ood) Down is a delightful 2D cooking game where players take on the role of a chef fulfilling customer orders. The challenge lies in preparing the necessary ingredients, cooking them to perfection, and serving the dishes promptly. What sets this game apart is its unique approach to difficulty—instead of the typical life system or timer, players face the intriguing twist of managing a stress level threshold. Success hinges on your ability to keep your cool and manage stress amidst the chaos of a bustling kitchen. Calm the F(ood) Down not only offers an enjoyable gaming experience but also serves as a valuable lesson in stress management within a dynamic and engaging environment.
We've developed a comprehensive time management mobile application designed to help users effectively track their activities, including focusing, cooking, exercising, meditating, and coding. Our app not only offers pre-defined categories but also empowers users to create custom tasks and monitor their time allocation. Much like the 'Screen Time' feature on iOS devices, our application generates insightful weekly graphs that showcase how users spend their time on various tasks. This feature enables users to gain valuable insights into their productivity and time management habits. The application is built using a robust tech stack, including Express.js, MongoDB, Node.js, and React Native, ensuring a seamless and efficient user experience.
Our motivation behind creating the Second Chance app was to provide a seamless and supportive transition for individuals seeking refuge in a new country, particularly amidst the worsening global refugee crisis. We recognized the importance of offering the best possible support to those in need. To achieve this, we developed the application using the powerful MERN stack. On the frontend, we harnessed the capabilities of React in conjunction with Material UI to craft a user-friendly and intuitive interface. Meanwhile, our backend was meticulously constructed using a combination of Express and MongoDB, complemented by the integration of various APIs. This robust setup was orchestrated to run smoothly on a Node.js runtime. Our aim is to empower refugees with a digital tool that eases their transition and enhances their access to essential resources and assistance.
